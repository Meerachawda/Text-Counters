Privacy Policy

At Text Counters, your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Text Counters is designed to be fully client-side, meaning all processing happens in your browser.

2. No Cookies

Our site does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. You can use Text Counters without worrying about hidden data collection.

3. Third-Party Services

Text Counters does not use any third-party analytics, ad networks, or plugins that collect data from you. However, links to external websites may be present, and their privacy policies may differ.

4. Text Input Security

All text entered into our tool remains entirely on your device. We do not transmit your data to any server, and no text is saved or logged.

5. Children's Privacy

This site is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from minors.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. If we make changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, feel free to contact the website administrator.
